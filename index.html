FAQ

Do I need a phone to use Telewise?

Only for the initial login — scan a QR code with your phone's Telegram app once. After that, Telewise runs fully standalone on your watch with Wi-Fi or LTE.

How do I log in?

Open Telewise on your watch, scan the QR code with your phone's Telegram app, and you're in.

Which watches are supported?

Any watch running Wear OS 3.0 or later (e.g., Google Pixel Watch, Samsung Galaxy Watch 4+).

Is my data safe?

Yes. Telewise uses Telegram's official TDLib library. Your messages go directly to Telegram's servers. We don't collect, store, or share any personal data.

Can I use it with Telegram bots?

Absolutely. Chat with any Telegram bot — Claude Code, OpenClaw, BotFather, CI/CD bots, and more — right from your wrist.

Do notifications work in the background?

Yes. Telewise uses Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver notifications even when the app is in the background. Just make sure the app hasn't been force-stopped.

Can I get early access to new features?

We're looking for dogfooders to help shape the app. Reach out if you're interested in testing upcoming features and providing feedback.
